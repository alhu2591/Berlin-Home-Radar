# HomeSync Berlin Source Catalog

This build expands the Berlin source catalog into a broad, city-scoped superset of verified housing websites across these groups:

- Main portals: ImmobilienScout24, Immowelt, Immonet, Wohnungsboerse
- Rooms and informal supply: WG-Gesucht, Kleinanzeigen
- Public housing / municipal supply: inberlinwohnen, degewo, GESOBAU, HOWOGE, STADT UND LAND, WBM, Gewobag
- Student supply: studierendenWERK BERLIN, Studentendorf Berlin, BGG Berlinovo student apartments
- Furnished / temporary supply: berlinovo Apartments, Wunderflats, HousingAnywhere, Spotahome, coming home, Urbanbnb
- Custom feeds: Local Agency RSS / Atom

Notes:

- Berlin-only scope is preserved across the app.
- Some websites expose direct listing/search pages and are marked active.
- Some websites are included as worker-ready templates because they may require user-provided URLs, slower parsing, or gentler operational handling.
- The catalog is intentionally broader than the enabled default source set.
